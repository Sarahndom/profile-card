About Me

My Bio

Hey there — I’m a frontend developer who loves bringing ideas to life on the web. For me, coding isn’t just about writing lines of code; it’s about creating experiences that feel smooth, intuitive, and enjoyable to use. I’m drawn to the visual side of tech clean layouts, thoughtful interactions, and design that just feels right. I enjoy experimenting with new tools and frameworks, learning something new every day, and finding better ways to make the web more accessible and engaging. At the end of the day, I just love building things that people enjoy using.

My Goals in This Program

  • My first goal is to strengthen my understanding of core web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and learn how to build responsive, user-friendly interfaces.
  • My second goal is to learn modern frontend tools and frameworks such as React, and understand how to work efficiently with APIs and real-world projects.
  • My third goal is to connect with other developers, share ideas, and gain mentorship that will help me grow into a confident and creative web developer.

Areas of Low Confidence
